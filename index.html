In vitro reconstitution: Cortex reorganization by myosin

How do biological membranes enable functions across scales?
We combine molecular reconstitution with quantitative cell and tissue imaging to study the following topics:

From molecules to organelles: How is the lipid composition of organelles controlled?
From cells to tissue: How do cell interfaces control and sense tissue mechanics and shape?
From tissue to organs: How are fluid cavities (lumen) formed and maintained?
